What is Coco Game?

Coco Game is a plant game that can develop good habits. Your learning time change coco grow time. The more learning time, the larger growing coco tree. We're committed to building a game about learning, and making players addicted to learning.Things like planting trees that require patience and persistence are especially helpful for you to develop good habits, just like you actually raise plants, except that they don't cost and you get a tree and a monetary reward when you finish planting a tree.


With our joint plant protection organization, you will also have the opportunity to participate in adventures from the jungle, completing activities you can get medals and certificates. You can share experiences with some players and exchange life.
